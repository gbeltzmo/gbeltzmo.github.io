Astrophysics PhD candidate at Vanderbilt University
Scroll to learn more about my research.

Research

I study the large scale structure of the Universe. In my research, I use a combination of large high-resolution N-body simulations and Halo Occupation Distribution models to learn about both cosmology and the galaxy-halo connection.
